Conceptdesign 1

In a project i was in we created conceptdesigns. Here are the first concept design. I made these Storyboards to visualize the future product. Here is a concept called ICA fast and go. The customer only has to scan the code on the shopping cart to start shpping. The shopping cart works lika a weight and can recognize the item being placed in it. If the customer puts an apple in, the shopping cart will understand it´s an apple and register it in the system. All concepts in the storyboard are designed to eliminate frustration in action and promote a safe, easy and fast way to trade.

Conceptdesign 2

This is the following of the Storyboard but with another concept. This concept make it eaiser for the custom to choose candy and the amounth but the most important value in this concept is the security measure. This concept is focused on preventing the spread of bacteria. It prevents people from touching or sneezing at the candy.

Conceptdesign 3

This is the following of the Storyboard but another concept. Here the customer will be able to pay through the mobile. Since the customer had linked the mobile with the shopping cart from the beginning and has, among other things, their shopping list on the mobile, the idea is to gather the entire customer experience through the mobile, this to avoid frustration and different methods through the purchase. But the customer also has the option of the usual method of payment by card.
